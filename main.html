summary

"Inception" is a mind-bending science fiction movie directed by Christopher Nolan. The story follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who specializes in entering people's dreams to steal their secrets. He is hired for a unique job - not to steal, but to implant an idea into someone's subconscious. The film explores the concept of dream manipulation and blurs the lines between reality and the dream world.

Actors

The movie features a talented ensemble cast, including Leonardo DiCaprio, Joseph Gordon-Levitt, Ellen Page, Tom Hardy, and Marion Cotillard.
